CHICKEN BIRYANI

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken (500g)
  • Basmati Rice (2 cups)
  • Onion (2 sliced)
  • Yogurt (½ cup)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste (1 tbsp)
  • Biryani Masala (2 tsp)
  • Mint Leaves
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Ghee (2 tbsp)
  • Salt (to taste)

RECIPE

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice until it is about 70% done and drain the water.
  4. Heat ghee in a pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is nearly done.
  6. Layer the cooked rice over the chicken.
  7. Sprinkle fried onions, mint leaves, and coriander leaves on top.
  8. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 15 to 20 minutes.
  9. Gently mix the rice and chicken before serving.
  10. Serve hot with raita or salad.
