BUTTER CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken (500g)
  • Butter (3 tbsp)
  • Tomato Puree (1 cup)
  • Fresh Cream (½ cup)
  • Onion (1 finely chopped)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste (1 tbsp)
  • Butter Chicken Masala (2 tsp)
  • Kasuri Methi (1 tsp)
  • Red Chili Powder (1 tsp)
  • Salt (to taste)

RECIPE

  1. Clean and cut the chicken into medium-sized pieces.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until lightly browned.
  4. Remove the chicken and set it aside.
  5. In the same pan, sauté the chopped onion until soft and golden.
  6. Add tomato puree and butter chicken masala, then cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked chicken to the gravy and mix well.
  8. Pour in the fresh cream and sprinkle kasuri methi.
  9. Simmer on low heat for 10 to 15 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  10. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
