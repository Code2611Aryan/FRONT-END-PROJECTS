PANNER BUTTER MASALA

INGREDIENTS

  • Paneer (250g)
  • Butter (3 tbsp)
  • Fresh Cream (½ cup)
  • Tomato Puree (1 cup)
  • Onion (1 finely chopped)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste (1 tbsp)
  • Cashew Paste (2 tbsp)
  • Red Chili Powder (1 tsp)
  • Garam Masala (1 tsp)
  • Kasuri Methi (1 tsp)
  • Turmeric Powder (½ tsp)
  • Coriander Powder (1 tsp)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (1 tbsp)
  • Fresh Coriander (for garnish)

RECIPE

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn soft and golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw aroma disappears.
  4. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Mix in the cashew paste and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Pour in fresh cream and stir well to create a rich, smooth gravy.
  8. Add paneer cubes and gently mix so they are coated with the gravy.
  9. Sprinkle Kasuri Methi and simmer for 3 to 5 minutes on low heat.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice.
