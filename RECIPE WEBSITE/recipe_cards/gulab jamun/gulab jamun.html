GULAB JAMUN

INGREDIENTS

  • Milk Powder (1 cup)
  • All-Purpose Flour (¼ cup)
  • Baking Soda (¼ tsp)
  • Ghee (2 tbsp)
  • Milk (as needed)
  • Sugar (1½ cups)
  • Water (1½ cups)
  • Cardamom Powder (½ tsp)
  • Rose Water (1 tsp)
  • Oil or Ghee (for frying)

RECIPE

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Add ghee and a little milk to form a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.
  4. In a pan, combine sugar and water to prepare the sugar syrup.
  5. Add cardamom powder and rose water, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Heat oil or ghee on low to medium heat for frying.
  7. Fry the dough balls until they turn evenly golden brown.
  8. Remove the fried jamuns and drain excess oil.
  9. Soak the warm jamuns in the warm sugar syrup for 1 to 2 hours.
  10. Serve warm or chilled and enjoy delicious Gulab Jamun.
